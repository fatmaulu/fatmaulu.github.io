Let's meet first

Hello, my name is Fatma Ulusoy. I was born on June 5, 2001 in Sinop. I have always been intertwined with art, but my real encounter with art started with my education at Sinop Fine Arts High School. As a result of four years of education, I participated in many exhibitions, including 20 exhibitions and 1 personal exhibition. I am currently studying at Ankara Hacı Bayram Veli University, Faculty of Art and Design, in the third year.
